                      AMAZON SPONSORED ADS

                      Here's a Step by step guide on to create Ads on Amazon Seller Central:Automaitc Campaign

                      Note that this is a basic guide from my online course since I don't have an actual Amazon Seller Central access.

                      1. Log in to Amazon Seller Central Account
                      2. Hover cursor over "advertising" and select "Campaign Manager from the drop-down menu
                      3. Click "Create Campaign"
                      4. Click "Continue" underneath Sponsored Products
                      5. Enter Product Name and Date
                      6. Enter a start date for campaign, then enter your daily budget
                      7. Click the circle next to "Automatic targeting"
                      8. Choose Client's preferred bidding strategy
                      9. Enter a name for your ad group similar to your campaign name
                      10. Scroll down to the "Products" section, then click "Add" next to the product for which you will be running this campaign
                      11. Enter your default bid or Set bids by targeting group (depends on Client's strategy)
                      12. Launch Campaign
